YOMI

Path of the Dead

The Magatama was stolen into the underworld. You follow— blades in hand, breath measured—across stones that float on black water. The dead do not forgive haste.

How to Play

Reach each torii with wakizashi reclaimed. On stratum 16, claim the Magatama and escape. You act—then the dead answer.

Footwork

  • Ayumi — step to a neighboring stone (green)
  • Hishō — bound farther (blue); costs Ki
  • Nadegiri — path brushes a yokai on both sides → cut
  • Tsuki — advance toward a foe with a gap between; katana thrusts (never step onto them)

Techniques

  • Keri — drive a yokai back; black water kills
  • Nage — throw the wakizashi; reclaim it to exit
  • Shrine — stand beside it, click once per stratum
  • Tamerau — skip a turn (if gifted)

Yokai

  • Oni — adjacent club
  • Tengu — line arrows
  • Bakemono — bombs
  • Onryō — fire beams

Remember

  • Cuts come from where you stand, not a separate attack
  • Ki returns beside threats, or from blood
  • No second life — one mistake ends the run
  • 1 Ayumi · 2 Keri · 3 Nage · Esc pause
